Fish Auction

The Details

Where: Auction Hall

When:

  • Item Registration: 8:30 - 11:00 AM
  • Bidder Registration: 8:30 - 11:59 AM
  • Killifish Classic Auction: 11:30 AM - 3:00 PM

See it, want it, buy it!

Hundreds of unique fish, beautiful tank fixtures and plants, and specialized yet affordable supplies. Our hobbyists and vendors will be putting all variety of items on display for purchase to the highest bidder. If you're buying, you're bound to get a fair price on highly-desired goods. If you're selling, you might get a lot more than you imagined for that unique or rare species you've been breeding. No matter who you are, it's going to be an exciting time.

Enter Fish, Plants, or Products in the Auction

To enter your goods the auction:

  1. Go to MyGroupAuctions.com. This site provides online help for the required processes of registering the entrant, joining a group (Aquarium Club of Lancaster County), adding items to an auction (Killifish Classic 2020 ACLC/CCY), and printing labels for your auction items.
  2. Bring your auction items to the auction desk for check-in, 8:00 – 11:00 Sunday!. All auction items must have a MyGroupAuctions label. Event staff will assist you from there.

Bid in the Auction

To buy fish, come to the auction on Sunday, starting at 11:00 AM and going until it’s over. You can see what will be in the auction by clicking the binoculars on the Killifish Classic 2020 line. (The closer to auction time the more complete this list will be). Check in at the door and get a bidder’s card, which will let you run a tab for your purchases, and pay by credit card if you so desire.

  1. Also consider visiting the in-room sales table in the vendor room on Saturday to see who is selling fish from their hotel room.
  2. As always, our vendors will be in the vendor room with the goods you know and many you don't. Available at all times during the show.
