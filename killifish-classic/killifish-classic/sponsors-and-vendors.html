Sponsors and Vendors

Sponsors

Please consider becoming a sponsor! Our sponsors make this event possible through generous donations.
There are many ways to sponsor the event. Become a sponsor at the Diamond, Gold, or Silver levels. Sponsors are recognized for their generosity:

  • Diamond: Large banner in atrium, large table in vendor hall, premium position on website. Contribute $750.
  • Gold: Medium banner in atrium, medium table in vendor hall, high position on website. Large Contribute $500.
  • Silver: Shared banner in atrium, medium table in vendor hall, displayed on website. Contribute $250.
  • Bronze: Shared banner in atrium, displayed on website. Contribute $150.

To become a sponsor, email us.

Vendors

Our event is perfect for vendors: thousands of aquarium enthusiasts walking our convention center all weekend long! This exposure puts your product front and center in front of an excited group of hobbyists. Help them acheive their visions by introducing them to your product.
Vendors are situated in the Vendor Room, connected directly to the atrium and the aquarium hall.

  • Book a large table: 16ft x 3ft. $400 for the weekend.
  • Book a medium table: 8ft x 3ft. $225 for the weekend.

To become a vendor, email us.
