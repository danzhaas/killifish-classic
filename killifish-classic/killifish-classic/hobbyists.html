Hobbyists Will Think They're In Heaven

There is so much for hobbyists to do at the Killifish Classic:

Show & Swap

  • Visit the Aquatic Show: Tour the hundreds of aquariums from dozens of fellow hobbyists. Admission is FREE!
  • Participate in the Aquatic show: Bring your best fish and show them off to thousands of fish enthusiasts.
  • Give A Kid A Tank Giveaway: We're giving away a tank to every interested child under the age of 16 (with parental consent). The perfect way to nourish an interest in fish in your own spawn!
  • Killifish Challenge: Enter your very best fish in the Killifish Challenge. Do your fish have what it takes to be best in show?
  • Fish Auction: Hundreds of unique fish, beautiful tank fixtures and plants, and specialized yet affordable supplies up for grabs to the highest bidder.
