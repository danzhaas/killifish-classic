Privacy Policy

Collection of Information

The information we collect falls broadly into the categories of browser information, personal email address, and interest form data. Browser information includes only referring site, date and time of visitor request, and additional websites requested. Your email is collected only if you voluntarily enter it in the newsletter sign-up form. The interest form data, like the email, is collected only if voluntarily entered in the newsletter sign up form. We do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Usage of Information

Browser information is used to understand user interests and needs related to the hobby only. This is used to better focus our site on the wants and needs of visitors. Individuals’ browser information is not sold or shared to or with any parties, including advertisers.
Interest form data is used to determine where visitors are referred from. This information is used to better determine which of our websites’ public relations strategies are most effective. It is not sold or shared to or with any parties, including advertisers.
Personal email address is used only to send email on requested topics related to the event to those individuals who request that content.

Third Party Information Usage

Our store uses Paypal for donations and purchases. Their privacy policies apply to any such transactions. Their policy may be viewed in its entirety here.

Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies. It uses them to help you log in to faster if you have used it before and to monitor analytics (information about which site referred you here and which pages here you visited). You can disable cookies on your device by disabling them in your browser settings or preferences.
