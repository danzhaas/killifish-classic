Introducing the Killifish Classic

The BIGGEST Killifish aquarium show on the East Coast

Hundreds of aquarium hobbyists show their prized fish. at the Clarion Hotel and Conference Center in New Cumberland, PA on Sept 13-15, 2020. This is the best place to see, show, buy, and appreciate killifish and aquariums. Entry is free!

Visit for Free

Participate in the show!

  1. See event schedule. See our schedule for when we open, when to see your favorite speaker, and when you can buy and sell fish in the auction.
  2. Plan your visit. Scenic New Cumberland and the Clarion Hotel are proud to host. Locate us, get directions, and learn the best places to stay.
  3. Register as a visitor. See our amazing aquatic show for FREE, enter the tank giveaway, and buy or sell in the auction.
  4. Accept the challenge. Show off your fish in the Killifish Challenge. Do your fish have what it takes to be best in show?
  5. Become a sponsor or vendor. Our sponsors and vendors are seen by thousands and help make the magic happen.
  6. Visit our web store. Show how much you love killifish and aquariums with our apparel, and buy tickets for special events.
